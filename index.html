FAQ

Answers to the things you're wondering.

How soon can you arrive?

For standard services we usually offer same-day or next-day slots. Deep cleans are best booked 1–2 days in advance.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes — our teams carry modern equipment, microfibre cloths, and eco-safe products. You just need to be home.

Is the staff verified?

Every professional is background-checked, trained, and in uniform. You can verify their ID on arrival.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a free re-clean within 24 hours if anything is missed. Your happiness is the job.

Which areas do you serve?

Nanakramguda and everywhere within 15 km — Gachibowli, Kokapet, Financial District, Manikonda, Narsingi, Kondapur, Madhapur & more.
